The historic Clover Cliff Ranch is set atop rolling tall grass prairies in the picturesque Flint Hills region. The working cattle ranch has been in operation since 1867, and the original homestead was built in 1860 from field stones on the property.

Travel

By Plane

For those traveling by plane, you can choose between two airports. We suggest renting a car once you arrive, as this is the cheapest option to and from the airport. However, car services are available.

Kansas City (MCI)

The larger airport with more flights. If you have time to spare, we recommend spending an extra day or two in KC on your way in or out to take in the sights, culture, and food. It is about a 2.5 hours to the venue, but well worth the drive! Stop at Guy and Mae’s Tavern in Williamsburg, KS for some of Megan’s favorite BBQ along the way.

Wichita Dwight D. Eisenhower National Airport (ICT)

A smaller regional airport, but only about 1.25 hours from the venue. Delta, United, American and Southwest Airlines all offer flights to ICT.

By Car

Clover Cliff Ranch is located approx. 3 miles southwest of Elmdale, KS on US-50 Hwy.
